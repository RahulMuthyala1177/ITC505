Welcome to Flagstaff

Flagstaff is a charming city located in northern Arizona, surrounded by beautiful mountains and forests.

Discover Natural Beauty

Explore the wonders of the Grand Canyon, hike through the scenic trails of Coconino National Forest, or ski on the slopes of Arizona Snowbowl.

Immerse Yourself in Culture

Visit the Museum of Northern Arizona to learn about the rich Native American history and art, or stroll through downtown Flagstaff to experience its vibrant arts scene.

Enjoy Outdoor Adventures

From mountain biking and rock climbing to kayaking and fishing, Flagstaff offers endless outdoor activities for adventure seekers of all ages.
